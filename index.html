Top Products & Solutions

Solar Water Heater

This Solar Water Heater comes with electric coil as well and cost-effective way to generate hot water for your home. Solar water heaters use natural sun light to heat water which is effective way to generate hot water without relying on eletricity. It is designed to provide hot water without consuming more electricity with the help of electric coil.

Inverter & Battery

We deal with Inverter, UPS and Batteries. An inverter is a device that converts battery power (DC) into alternating current (AC) of a higher voltage. This means that most inverters are installed and used in conjunction with a battery bank. This is enough to store energy in the form of direct current and the home appliances.

RO Water Plant

RO Purifier system and Water Filters are water purification system, which helps to separate the dissolved contaminants and harmful chemicals for generating only clean water. This plant helps to generate water at large scale safe for consumption and reduces the risk of water-borne diseases. This system has capacity to store 12 Litre to 25 Litre.

Water Level Controller

SKR-Tech's powerful automatic water level indicator and controlling system. It functions to a control water level and regulate water flow and optimize system performance. Automatic device that manages water levels on a variety of systems such as water tanks, pumps and swimming pools.

Water Purifier

Water Filters is a water purification filter. It helps to remove unwanted impurities from water such as sediment, taste and odour, hardness and bacteria to result in better quality water. Filters unpleasant tastes and odours from chemicals such as chlorine which are used to disinfect the water and keep it free of germs and bacteria.

Water Softner

A Water softener can be a lifesaver for homeowners plagued by the effects of hard water. Reduces Lime Build-up on Plumbing Fixtures, Allows Water Heater to Warm Up Water Faster, Maintains Softer Clothing and by reducing Less Soap & Detergent.
